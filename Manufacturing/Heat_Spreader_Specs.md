# Pi 5 Thermal Spreader Plate
To be 'stolen' from a Pi 400 or custom-cut from 1.5mm Aluminum.

## Dimensions
- **Width:** 250mm
- **Depth:** 100mm
- **Material:** 6061 Aluminum

## Assembly
1. Place Thermal Pad (1.5mm thickness) on Pi 5 SoC.
2. Bolt Pi 5 to the metal plate using M2.5 standoffs.
3. Secure plate to the 'universal_mount_grid' in the OpenSCAD file.
